#### Is OpenFit 2 a bone conduction headphone?

No, OpenFit 2 is an air conduction headphone. It uses DualBoost™ Technology to deliver vibrant and balanced audio.

#### What scenarios is OpenFit 2 suitable for?

OpenFit 2 offers a comfortable and secure fit, and its open-ear design allows you to stay aware of your surroundings. It’s ideal for various scenarios, including running, working out, office work, and relaxing at home.

#### Is OpenFit 2 sweat-resistant?

Yes, OpenFit 2 features an IP55 water-resistance rating, protecting it from low-pressure water splashes and sweat while ensuring reliable performance, even during intense workouts or rainy conditions.

#### What is the battery life of OpenFit 2?

OpenFit 2 offers up to 11 hours of battery life on a single charge. When paired with the charging case, the total battery life extends to 48 hours. However, actual battery performance may vary depending on volume, audio source, ambient noise, and usage habits.

#### Does OpenFit 2 come in different sizes?

Currently, OpenFit 2 is available in one universal size. Its ergonomic design ensures a comfortable and secure fit, offering flexibility to accommodate a variety of ear shapes.

#### What music genres are best suited for OpenFit 2?

OpenFit 2 offers four customizable EQ modes via the Shokz App, each one tailored to different music preferences: ● Standard Mode: Offers balanced sound with strong vocals and adequate bass. Ideal for most songs and users seeking a well-rounded listening experience. ● Vocal Mode: Offers enhanced vocal clarity with reduced bass. Perfect for podcasts, audiobooks, and vocal-heavy tracks. ● Bass Boost Mode: Offers powerful bass with a heavier feel. Best for users focused solely on low frequencies. ● Treble Boost Mode: Offers enhanced high-frequency details and spatial clarity. Well-suited for classical, light music, or female vocals.

#### Do I need to remove the protective film on the charging case？

Yes. Each OpenFit 2 charging case comes with two small protective films near the charging contacts. For optimal charging performance, please remove the films before using the product. Rest assured, removing them will not affect the lifespan of your earbuds or their warranty coverage.

#### How is OpenFit 2 controlled?

OpenFit 2 combines physical buttons and touch controls for greater precision and ease of use. The physical multifunction buttons prevent accidental touches caused by sweat. Users can also customize button functions. The voice assistant function is turned off, and you can use the Shokz App to turn on touch control, then wake it up with a touch long press.

#### Can OpenFit 2 be used with a single earbud?

Yes, both earbuds can be used independently. You can listen to music or take calls with one earbud. When used individually, each earbud maintains its respective button functionality.

#### If I take the earbuds out of the case and put them on without any music playing, will the battery life drain at all or will it remain at full battery until I start using them?

Yes, the earbuds will slowly lose power. They can last for approximately 2.5 days in this state. If stored in the charging case with the case turned off, the earbuds can last up to 270 days.

#### At what point does OpenFit 2 enter standby mode?

When the earbuds are outside the charging case and disconnected from Bluetooth devices for more than 3 minutes, they will enter standby mode to preserve battery life.

#### What should I do if I lose my OpenFit 2 earbuds?

You can use the "Find My Earbuds" feature in the Shokz App. With one click, the earbuds will emit a sound, helping you easily locate them.

#### How do I store my OpenFit 2?

1. Before charging, ensure that the charging port is dry and free of any liquid build-up, such as sweat. Use a soft, dry cloth to wipe away any moisture or residue from the charging port. 2. Gently wipe the earbuds using a soft, clean, dry cloth. When cleaning the earbud mesh, gently brush away dirt and earwax with a dry cotton swab, then wipe with a soft cloth. 3. Use a soft, clean and dry cloth to wipe the exterior of the charging case and a brush to remove debris from the charging interface. Gently wipe the charging contact surface inside the charging case using a dry cotton swab.

#### What are the differences between Shokz Bone Conduction and TWS products

● Bone Conduction Series: Focused on sports users, these headphones provide secure, comfortable, and stable wear during intense activities. Compared to TWS, bone conduction headphones excel in stability, ease of wear, waterproofing, sweat resistance, button functionality, and battery life. ● Open-Earbuds TWS Series: These are designed for everyday use across all scenarios, including home, office, and workouts. Open-Earbuds feature an open, comfortable, and stable fit without in-ear design, offering advantages in comfort, portability, and sound quality.

#### What are the differences between OpenFit 2 and OpenFit?

OpenFit 2 is a fully upgraded version of OpenFit. It features DualBoost™ Technology for superior sound quality. Additionally, OpenFit 2 improves on comfort, battery life, and water resistance compared to its predecessor. The new model also introduces physical multifunction buttons for greater precision and convenience and adds a "Find My Earbuds" feature, allowing users to easily locate their earbuds through the Shokz App.

#### How does sound quality compare between OpenFit 2, OpenFit, and OpenFit Air?

● OpenFit 2: Utilizes DualBoost™ Technology with independent high- and low-frequency drivers, delivering stronger bass, clearer highs, and richer details compared to OpenFit and OpenFit Air. ● OpenFit & OpenFit Air : Both feature a single customized high-sensitivity driver. OpenFit is equipped with an upgraded Openbass™1.0 algorithm, offering better sound quality than OpenFit Air.Overall, all three models perform well against market standards for open-ear TWS headphones.

#### How does comfort level compare between OpenFit 2, OpenFit, and OpenFit Air?

● OpenFit 2 : Utilizes the latest self-developed Shokz Ultra-Soft Silicone™ 2.0, providing superior comfort for all-day wear. Its flexible ear hook, inspired by a golden spiral, is designed to optimize gravity distribution for a better, secure fit. ● OpenFit: Uses Shokz Ultra-Soft Silicone™ 1.0 to offer a comfortable wearing experience, with a pliable fit for every ear shape. Its Special Dolphin Arc Ear Hook is designed for a secure fit, gently adapting to the natural shape of your ear. ● OpenFit Air: Features an Air Ear Hook and an ergonomic dual-bevel design, ensuring a secure and snug fit. Its 25.5° comfort angles contribute to a lighter-than-air feel that practically defies movement.

#### Do I need to download an App to use OpenFit 2?

While OpenFit 2 works seamlessly without an app, downloading the Shokz App is recommended for an enhanced experience. The App allows you to switch EQ modes, connect to two devices, customize button functions, locate your earbuds, adjust prompt volume, and keep track of firmware updates.

#### How can OpenFit 2 connect to two devices simultaneously?

a. Start by connecting the earbuds to your first device via Bluetooth, then open the Shokz App. Select the "MultiPoint Pairing" option. b. On the pop-up page, confirm to open the MultiPoint Pairing function. c. Select "Connect to the Second Device" and follow the instructions to establish a connection with the second device. d. Your earbuds are now connected to two devices. You can manage their Bluetooth connections conveniently through the Shokz App.

#### With the “Find My Earbuds” function, do I have to be within a certain distance from the headphones for it to work?

The feature works effectively if your earbuds are within a range of 10 meters from your phone. Actual Bluetooth transmission speed and range may vary depending on environmental factors, product features, and usage habits. For the most accurate performance, please refer to your actual usage conditions.
