Shokz DirectPitch™ Technology

Shokz DirectPitch™ technology uses reverse sound waves to optimise the distance and angle between the sound source and the human ear, resulting in relatively greater sound pressure toward the human ear and less sound pressure in directions other than towards the ear canal.

This allows the listener to hear the sound without headphones being in the ear, and also has the advantage of reducing sound leakage and ensuring privacy.

## FAQ

1.Can I wear just one earbud, or are both needed?

Both earbuds are designed for independent use, allowing for mono music playback or calls. When used individually, each earbud maintains its respective button functionality.

2.Can I purchase the charging case/earbud separately?

Sorry, we do not sell the charging case/earbud separately.

3. Under what circumstance will OpenFit 2 enter standby mode?

When the earbuds are outside the charging case and remain disconnected from Bluetooth devices for 3 minutes, they will enter standby mode to preserve battery life.

4. Can I wear glasses and OpenFit 2 together?

Definitely you can. OpenFit 2 is designed friendly to wear with glasses.
