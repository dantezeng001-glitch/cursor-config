#### What is the difference between OpenSwim Pro and OpenSwim?

1. OpenSwim Pro supports both Bluetooth® connectivity and MP3 Mode, with expanded 32GB of storage. OpenSwim supports MP3 playback only, with 4GB of storage and no Bluetooth functionality.2. OpenSwim Pro features PremiumPitch™ 2.0+, Shokz’s 8th-generation Bone Conduction Technology, which reduces vibration and sound leakage while delivering improved audio quality compared to the 7th-generation technology used in OpenSwim.3. In terms of battery life, OpenSwim Pro offers up to 9 hours in Bluetooth Mode and 6 hours in MP3 Mode, while OpenSwim provides up to 8 hours of playback.4. OpenSwim Pro also charges faster, reaching a full charge in approximately 1.5 hours, about 30 minutes faster than OpenSwim.

#### How much MP3 storage does OpenSwim Pro have, and how many songs can it hold?

A: OpenSwim Pro has 32GB of MP3 storage. The estimated capacity can vary depending on the file size of your songs. Here's a breakdown for your reference: Regular Quality Songs (around 4 MB each): You can store approximately 8,000 songs. High-Quality Songs (around 8MB each): You can store approximately 4,000 songs.

#### What music file formats are compatible with OpenSwim Pro? Does it support Bluetooth music transfer?

A: OpenSwim Pro offers broad compatibility with various audio formats, including MP3, FLAC, WAV, AAC, M4A, APE, and WMA. However, please note that OpenSwim Pro does not support Bluetooth music transfer. You can upload your music files directly to the headphones using the included 4-Pin Magnetic Charging Cable. For detailed instructions, please refer to our ["How-To Video".](https://youtu.be/gZR-JnRhRZ8)

#### Does OpenSwim Pro automatically disconnect from a Bluetooth device when switching to MP3 mode?

A: No, OpenSwim Pro maintains its Bluetooth connection even when you switch to MP3 mode. This allows you to seamlessly transition back and forth between your music library stored on the device and Bluetooth streaming without needing to re-pair your headphones.

#### What is the battery life of OpenSwim Pro in Bluetooth mode vs. MP3 mode? Does battery life vary depending on use environment (land vs. underwater)?

A: OpenSwim Pro offers up to 9 hours of battery life when using Bluetooth mode and up to 6 hours of battery life in MP3 mode. This is consistent regardless of whether you're using the headphones on land or underwater.

#### Does OpenSwim Pro come in different sizes?

A: No, OpenSwim Pro is currently available in a single, universal size.

#### Can I use Bluetooth mode underwater?

A: No, Bluetooth connectivity is not functional underwater with OpenSwim Pro. Water effectively disrupts Bluetooth signals, limiting underwater use to the MP3 mode with its internal storage.

#### Is OpenSwim Pro suitable for diving?

A: No, OpenSwim Pro is not recommended for diving. While the headphones feature a waterproof rating of up to 2 meters (6.5 feet), they are not intended for use at deeper depths. Additionally, we advise against using OpenSwim Pro in seawater. If your headphones come into contact with salt water, soak them in fresh water for about 30 minutes to remove salt.

#### Can I charge OpenSwim Pro with the OpenSwim charging cable?

A: No, OpenSwim Pro and OpenSwim utilize different charging cables. OpenSwim Pro requires the included 4-Pin Magnetic Charging Cable for proper charging.

#### Can OpenSwim Pro connect to a PC or TV

A: Yes, OpenSwim Pro’s Bluetooth connectivity allows you to pair it with various devices including PCs, TVs, mobile phones, smartwatches, and more.

#### Can I use OpenSwim Pro in seawater?

Yes. OpenSwim Pro can be used in seawater. To help prevent salt buildup, soak the headphones in fresh water for about 30 minutes after each use. Make sure the magnetic charging port is completely dry before charging or transferring data to ensure proper operation and safety.

#### How to download the Shokz App?

You can search "Shokz" on the App Store or Google Play to download the Shokz App.

#### What device and operating system does the Shokz App require?

Shokz App is only compatible with mobile phones and does not yet support other devices such as laptops, PCs, tablets/Pads, etc. Your mobile operating system requires at least iOS 13.0 / Android 8.0 or higher.

#### How to connect the Shokz App with OpenSwim Pro?

The steps are suitable for first connecting and after resetting. 1. Start with your OpenSwim Pro powered off. 2. Enter pairing mode by pressing and holding volume+ for 5-7 seconds. Your OpenSwim Pro will turn on (continue holding the button) and then the LED light will flash red and blue. Audrey will say "pairing" indicating pairing mode. 3. Open your device's Bluetooth settings and select "OpenSwim Pro by Shokz". Once your device confirms successful pairing, you're all set. 4. Open the Shokz App and follow the steps on the screen.

Download the Shokz App (version 5.6.0 or later) to customize your OpenSwim Pro. You can choose playback order, switch playlists, and enjoy optimized audio with the dedicated Swimming EQ Mode underwater. The app also lets you switch between Bluetooth® V5.4 and MP3 Mode, manage MultiPoint Pairing and EQ settings, view battery status, and receive firmware updates.
