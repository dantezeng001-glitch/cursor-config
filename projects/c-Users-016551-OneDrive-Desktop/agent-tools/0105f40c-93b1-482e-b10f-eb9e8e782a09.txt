Apple today introduced[new wireless over-ear headphones called AirPods Max](https://www.macrumors.com/2020/12/08/airpods-max/), and it has since shared the first ad for the headphones on its YouTube channel. The 90-second video is titled "Journey into Sound" and shows someone wearing AirPods Max in space.
[![Embedded YouTube video](https://img.youtube.com/vi/YZNearcOsXg/0.jpg)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YZNearcOsXg)
"Introducing AirPods Max," says Apple. "Computational audio. Adaptive EQ. Active Noise Cancellation. Transparency mode. Spatial audio."
Apple goes into more detail about the AirPods Max in a separate video narrated by company executives Evans Hankey, VP of Industrial Design, and Gary Geaves, VP of Acoustics. The video is set to the song "Empty Dancefloor" by Soulwax.
[![Embedded YouTube video](https://img.youtube.com/vi/FXI_-OesT3A/0.jpg)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FXI_-OesT3A)
"High-fidelity audio," says Apple. "Active Noise Cancellation with Transparency mode. Spatial audio for theater-like sound that surrounds you. Stunning design with an exceptional fit. All with the effortless magic of AirPods."

> > If you're looking for wireless, I have been really enjoying my Sony WH-1000XM3s. They've released the XM4s now, but it's one of those products that are so good that Sony decided not to over-do the improvements because why fix what isn't broke? My XM3s have terrific audio quality, they feel great, they have a longer battery than any of the airpods (including the new ones), the ANC works well. I've traveled all over the world with these and have loved them. They don't have apple's fancy pairing mechanism, but putting them into bluetooth pairing mode is super easy and the XM4s have multiple-device pairing.
