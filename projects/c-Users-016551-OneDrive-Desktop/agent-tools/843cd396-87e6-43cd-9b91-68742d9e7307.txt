Absolutely. The open-ear design of Mojawa Run Plus allows you to maintain 100% situational awareness. You can hear approaching vehicles, sirens, and ambient noises while enjoying your music, which is critical for road safety.

###### Can bone conduction headphones work underwater?

Yes, bone conduction headphones can work underwater. The sound vibrations transmit through the cheek and jaw bones instead of the ear canal. The Mojawa Run Plus can be submerged up to 2 meters underwater for up to 2 hours.

###### What should I do before swimming with my headphones?

For a better swimming experience, please follow these steps:

1. Disconnect Bluetooth connection on your phone and switch to MP3 mode on your headphones.

2. Disable the touchpad (press 4 times while in "power on" mode).

###### Can you still hear sound with bone conduction headphones?

Yes, you can still hear sound with bone conduction headphones. The sound vibrations are transmitted through the bones of your skull directly to your inner ear, bypassing the eardrum.

###### Can deaf people hear sound with bone conduction headphones?

Yes, deaf people can perceive sound through bone conduction headphones because they do not rely on the eardrum to transmit sound. The vibrations directly stimulate the cochlea. However, individuals with certain neurological impairments or disabilities may have different responses, depending on their specific condition and medical advice.

###### Is it disrespectful to wear headphones on a golf course, and how does Mojawa solve this?

Traditionally, wearing noise-canceling earbuds on a golf course is seen as a breach of golf etiquette because it isolates you from your playing partners and the environment. However, Mojawa bone conduction headphones offer the perfect compromise for the modern golfer:

- Social Connectivity: Because our open-ear design leaves your ear canals completely open, you can listen to your favorite podcast or low-volume background music while still engaging in crystal-clear conversation with your group. No more missing a "Nice putt!" or a "Fore!" shout from a neighboring fairway.
- Safety & Awareness: On the course, hearing is a safety feature. Mojawa allows you to hear approaching golf carts, maintenance equipment, and wind direction, ensuring you stay safe and aware of your surroundings at all times.
- All-Day Comfort: A typical round of 18 holes lasts 4 to 5 hours. Unlike in-ear buds that cause "ear fatigue" or sweat buildup in the Florida or Arizona heat, Mojawa sits lightly on your cheekbones, providing all-day comfort without the itchy or clogged feeling.
- Swing-Proof Stability: The titanium wraparound frame is engineered to stay secure during the most powerful drives and high-speed swings. It won’t fall out or shift, allowing you to focus entirely on your alignment and tempo.

###### Do bone conduction headphones leak sound?

Bone conduction headphones have minimal sound leakage compared to traditional headphones. The sound is localized and directed towards your head rather than outward.
