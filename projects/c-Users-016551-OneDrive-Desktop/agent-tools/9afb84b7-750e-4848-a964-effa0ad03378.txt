# About this item
* CONSUMER INSPIRED DESIGN - We took all of your feedback on our Original Tri Series and designed the Tri 2 with you in mind; featuring intuitive redesigned controls and a soft silicone finish for maximum comfort and control during any activity; The included hard shell protective case protects against knocks, impacts, bumps, drops, impacts and crushes and is equipped with an internal mesh pocket to keep your USB cable and accessories organized
* REVOLUTIONARY DUAL-MODE PERFORMANCE: Our most advanced bone conduction technology delivers vibrant, crystal-clear audio when switching between environments. Use Bluetooth 5.3 for streaming music, podcasts, or taking calls during any land activities. For underwater adventures, switch to MP3 mode to enjoy uninterrupted playback from the built-in 8GB memory, providing clear audio up to 12 feet underwater
* EXTENDED BATTERY LIFE & MEMORY: Whether you're enjoying your favorite music from memory or live-streaming the TRI 2 has you covered. The TRI 2 delivers an average of 8.5 hours of mixed-use playtime, 9 hours of bluetooth and 6 hours of MP3 playing, ensuring it will keep up with you and go the distance. With 8GB memory you can store 2000 songs and listen up to 130 hours of music. The TRI 2 supports the following audio formats, (M4A, MP3, WMA, FLAC, and APE)
* SMART, EASY CONTROLS: Enjoy effortless control with the TRI 2’s newly optimized button placement, designed for intuitive use even in challenging conditions. Easily adjust volume, skip tracks, or answer calls without missing a beat, providing a seamless experience for any activity, whether on land or underwater
* H2O BEYOND PROMISE: H2O Audio stands by the durability and performance of our products with a one-year "Go Beyond" promise. Register your product, and we'll add an additional year protection. * * * * ### Can I live stream using Bluetooth while underwater?
No, Bluetooth isn't functional underwater, we recommend directly downloading your favorite audio files for your aquatic adventures. Planning a swim? Easily download your favorite audio in multiple formats, including M4A, MP3, WMA, FLAC, and APE.
* ### How water-resistant or waterproof are these headphones?
These headphones boast beyond IPX8 waterproof technology and can be submerged up to 3 meters for an unlimited amount of time making them beyond waterproof and perfect for swimming and sweat resistance.
* ### What sports or activities are these headphones best suited for?
Built to seamlessly transition between different sports. Experience unparalleled audio versatility with these headphones. Designed for all your athletic endeavors, whether you're swimming, running, biking or padding the TRI 2 Series has you covered.
* ### Can these headphones withstand exposure to chlorine and saltwater?
Yes, these headphones have a closed build design and beyond IPX8 waterproof technology. We do recommend rinsing with fresh clean water after use in pools and saltwater environments.

*4 out of 5 stars*
##### Excellent sound, comfortable fit, good button placement. Proprietary charging cable.
Pros: This pair of bone conduction headphones have some of the best sound quality I've heard in this type. They are comfortable to wear for long periods; I can go hours without realizing they are there. I find them to be a perfect solution to being able to hear what's going on around me while still having a favorite podcast or audio book going. They connected quickly to my phone when I switched them to bluetooth mode although I kind of wish they stayed in that mode when powering off and powering on again. When I start them, they go into memory mode and begin playing the audio track describing how to use them. A quick double-click switches between modes, so it's easy to get into bt mode and my source player starts immediately. I also love the zipper hard case which holds them perfectly and offers great protection for storage in a backpack or bag. Cons: Probably in the pursuit of waterproofing, they went with a proprietary magnetic charger, which means if this cable gets lost of damaged, the headset becomes basically useless when the battery drains. A waterproof cover over a usb-c connection would be a lot better, but I suppose that would introduce another point of failure. Also, the auto power-off is a little too short. It should be closer to 10 minutes before powering down automatically. I think it's only about 3-5 minutes. They are a bit pricey and other than the waterproof, swimming benefits, I don't know that their sound quality justifies this higher price. At this price point, they should come with a spare charging cable and possibly a coupon for another replacement cable if and when this one gets lost or damaged. Overall, probably the best-sounding bc headphones I've tried to date, though still not as good as a full, over-ear ANC pair. Great for outdoor use when you need to hear surrounding sounds. Also great for driving to listen to something that other occupants of the car might not want to hear.
