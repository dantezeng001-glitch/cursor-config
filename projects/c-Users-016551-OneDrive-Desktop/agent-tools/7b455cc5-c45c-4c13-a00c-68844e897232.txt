# About this item

- Land & Water Use: Run Plus New [Upgraded] features an innovative design for both land and water sports. You can switch between Bluetooth mode (for running) and MP3 mode (for swimming, Bluetooth does not work underwater) with just one button. This phone-free listening experience keeps you motivated and energized throughout every workout. Once you switch to MP3 mode, you can enjoy songs stored locally on the generous 32GB internal storage, so all your favorite daily tunes are always with you.
- IP68 Waterproof: Run Plus New is the perfect wireless headset for swimming. With an IP68 waterproof rating, it easily handles sweaty runs, rainy workouts, and even underwater swimming up to 8.2 feet. It provides an immersive underwater music experience, delivering rich, high-quality sound while you swim laps.
- Enhanced Sound Quality: Endorsed by five Grammy-winning audio producers, the Run Plus New earphones deliver an unrivaled listening experience. Featuring our patented Physical Bass Strengthening Technology and Dual Suspension Anchor System, these headphones provide deep, rich bass, melodious mids, and intricate sound details throughout the whole audio spectrum.
- Multipoint Pairing & Fast Charging: Stay connected seamlessly with our Multipoint Pairing technology, allowing you to pair with two devices at once. The improved Auto-Reconnect feature ensures that if your signal is dropped due to obstacles or interference, it will link back up instantly. Plus, never let a low battery stop you—our Magnetic Fast Charging provides 80 minutes of playtime with just a 5-minute charge, making it perfect for your grab-and-go lifestyle.
- Open-Ear Safety & Comfort: Stay safe with our open-ear design that ensures maximum situational awareness during outdoor activities. Enjoy a bud-free, pain-free listening experience that lets you chat with others or hear traffic without removing your headset. This design promotes ear hygiene and protects your long-term hearing. Its lightweight, ergonomic shape stays secure and fits perfectly with swim caps, goggles, sunglasses, and helmets—the ultimate choice for swimming, running, and cycling.

### What should I do before swimming with my headphones?

Underwater: switch to MP3 Mode. Bluetooth will not work underwater.

Before swimming, adjust the volume and click the power button 4 times to lock it. This prevents accidental touches and ensures stable volume while in the water.

### How do I upload audio files to the Run Plus New?

Connect Run Plus New to your computer using the data cable's USB port. Wait for the Run Plus New drive to pop up, then drag and drop your audio files into the drive. Run Plus New is compatible with MP3, WAV, AAC, APE and FLAC file types.

### Is it possible to use Run Plus New with streaming services like Spotify and Apple Music?

Yes, you can use Spotify and Apple music on Run Plus New, but not on MP3 mode.

### Does the Run Plus New come with different playing modes?

In MP3 mode, Run Plus New offers shuffle, repeat, and normal modes. However, in Bluetooth mode, you'll need to manage the playback modes directly from your music player.

### Why do touch controls on headphones stop working when they get wet?

When moisture, such as sweat or rainwater, adheres to the touch-sensitive area, it can interfere with the touch sensor's recognition of touch signals, causing signal distortion and resulting in poor touch responsiveness or even loss of control.

### How does the Run Plus New improve upon the original Run Plus?

Run Plus New has upgraded the following: 1. Premium durable build for daily active use

2. Shatter & corrosion resistant

3. Water-resistant for all environments

4. Dual-device auto-reconnect

5. Faster, smoother pairing

### What can I do if it’s hard to hear my bone conduction headphones in loud places like airports, subways, or busy streets?

1. Open-ear design for safety & situational awareness

2. Includes premium noise-reducing earplugs

3. Enhances bone conduction audio for richer sound

4. Quick tip: Cover one ear for instant clarity in noise

### Why Choose Run Plus New Headphones?

Versatile performance across nearly any environment. Whether swimming, running, cycling, skiing, surfing, on land or water, for daily use or extreme sports—Run Plus New headphones deliver outstanding performance every time.

##### Recommend for: Swimmers, runners, cyclists, triathletes, anyone who loves outdoor/water sports!

I’ve been on the hunt for a reliable pair of Bone Conduction Swimming Headphones for months—one that works great in the pool and for my daily runs/cycling—and the Mojawa Run Plus New checks every single box! This upgraded model is a game-changer for anyone who loves water sports and land workouts, and I can’t say enough good things about it. First off, the IP68 waterproof rating is no joke. I swim laps 5 days a week, often going down to 8ft in the pool, and these headphones hold up perfectly—no water seepage, no glitches, just clear, immersive sound underwater. The one-button switch between Bluetooth (for land) and MP3 mode (for swimming) is so intuitive, and the 32GB internal storage is a huge win! I loaded all my favorite workout playlists, podcasts, and even audiobooks, and I never have to worry about bringing my phone to the pool. Uploading files is super easy too—just plug it into my laptop via USB-C, drag and drop, and done. It supports all major audio formats (MP3, WAV, FLAC, etc.), which is a nice touch for audiophiles like me. The sound quality blew me away for bone conduction tech! Thanks to the patented Physical Bass Strengthening Technology and Maglev-Bass actuator, the bass is deep and rich—way better than other bone conduction headphones I’ve owned. The mids are melodious and the sound details are crisp, even underwater. I also love the open-ear design: it keeps me safe on my morning runs and bike rides because I can hear traffic, other people, and my surroundings, but it’s still comfortable enough to wear for hours without any pain or pressure on my ears.
