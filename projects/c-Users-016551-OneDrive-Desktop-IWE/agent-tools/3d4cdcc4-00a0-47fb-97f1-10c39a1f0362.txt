4. How to charge
Get up to 6 hours on a single charge when 
playing music with ANC on, with a total charge 
of up to 27 hours with the Smart case. If you use 
the earbuds without ANC and HearThrough, 
you’ll get even more battery, with up to 8 hours 
in the earbuds when playing music, and a total 
of 36 hours with the Smart case.
4.1 Charging the earbuds
Place the earbuds in the Smart case and close 
the lid. It takes up to 3 hours to fully recharge 
the earbuds. 
When the Smart case is opened, the LED inside 
the Smart case will light up red, yellow or green 
to indicate the battery status of the earbuds. 
The LED on the front of the Smart case 
indicates the battery status of the Smart case.
11 ENGLISH
4.2 Fast charge the earbuds
When the earbuds are at low battery, placing 
them in the Smart case for 5 minutes will fast 
charge the earbuds and give up to 1 hour of 
music time. Fast charging the earbuds requires 
that the Smart case has a minimum of 30% 
battery.
You can see the current battery level of the 
earbuds and Smart case using the Jabra 
Sound+ app when the earbuds are connected.

7.7 Sidetone
Sidetone enables you to hear your own voice 
when you are on a call. The benefit of Sidetone 
is that it enables you to determine how loud 
you are speaking.
The earbuds automatically adapt in windy 
environments to ensure an optimal Sidetone 
experience.
Sidetone is on by default during calls and can 
be turned off/on and adjusted using the Jabra 
Sound+ app. 
By default, Sidetone cannot be turned off/on 
using the earbuds. However, it can be added to 
the earbuds using MyControls in the Jabra 
Sound+ app.

When one of the earbuds is removed from your 
ear, music will automatically pause.
To resume the music, return the earbud to your

7.11 Sleep mode
To conserve battery, the earbuds will 
automatically power off when they are 
disconnected from your mobile device for 15 
minutes and are not in the Smart case, or when 
not wearing them and they have been inactive 
for 30 minutes.
To exit sleep mode, press and hold (2 sec) the 
button on each earbud. Alternatively, place the 
earbuds in the Smart case and then remove 
them from the Smart case.
Sleep mode can be configured in the Jabra 
Sound+ app.

7.14 Bluetooth® LE audio
LE audio enhances the performance of 
standard Bluetooth® audio by providing lower 
power consumption and hi-fi sound. LE audio 
introduces AuracastTM for audio sharing with 
multiple wireless headsets.
The availability of LE audio may vary by device 
and may only be supported on selected phone 
models (Samsung and Google).
Use LE Audio with your Android phone
1. Ensure your Android phone is updated to the 
latest operating system (Android 15 or later).
2. Update your earbuds to the latest firmware 
using the Jabra Sound+ app.
3. Go to the Bluetooth menu on your Android 
phone, select Jabra Elite 10 Gen 2 from the 
list and delete the current pairing.
4. Re-pair your earbuds with your Android 
phone (refer to section 6.1).
5. Go to the Bluetooth settings of your Jabra 
Elite 10 Gen 2 on your Android phone and 
turn on LE Audio.
37 ENGLISH

(refer to section 6.2).

9.2 How to care for your earbuds
• Always store the earbuds in the Smart case 
and safely protected.
• To prevent the depletion of the battery 
lifetime or capacity, avoid storing the earbuds 
in hot or cold environments, such as a closed 
car in summer, or in winter conditions.
• It is recommended to store the earbuds 
between -20°C and 45°C (-4°F and 113°F).
• Do not store the earbuds for extended 
periods of time without recharging them 
(max. three months).
• If the earbuds or the Smart case become 
dirty, it is recommended to clean them with a 
soft, lint-free cloth that has been dampened 
with clean water.
• The Smart case uses the standard USB 
voltage of 5v +- 0.25v. It is not recommended 
to charge the earbuds using a power supply 
with a higher voltage.
• Do not remove the earbud silicone sleeve.

9.5 Using a replacement Smart case
Before using a replacement Smart case for the 
first time, it will need to be synced with your 
current earbuds and charged for 1 hour.
1. To begin syncing, place your current earbuds 
inside the replacement Smart case and close 
the lid for 10 seconds. 
2. If the LED on the Smart case flashes pink this 
indicates that the firmware is being updated. 
3. Wait until the LED stops flashing pink before 
opening the Smart case.
