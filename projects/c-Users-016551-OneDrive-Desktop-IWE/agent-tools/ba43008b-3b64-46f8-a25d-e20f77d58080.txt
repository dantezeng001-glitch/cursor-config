### Say goodbye to traditional in-ear headphones andhello to the open soundscape.
The OWS, unlike any other in-ear or open-type earbuds, boasts a patented open design that sits comfortably on your ear, delivering sound directly into your ear canal. This unique design lets you enjoy your music while staying aware of your surroundings, making it perfect for running and workouts.

### Extremecomfort and stability.
The oladance OWS stereo is crafted with soft, skin-friendly silicone and an extra-strong memory titanium wireframe. Designed to fit the natural curve of your ear, the OWS evenly distributes the weight across your ear, eliminating discomfort even during prolonged use.

### The world'sfirst open-ear headphoneswith an anti-hearing loss algorithm.
We are proud to introduce the world's first open-ear headphones with an anti-hearing loss algorithm. This patented technology simulates the working principle of the human ear's auditory ossicles, dynamically adjusting the sound energy based on your listening time, music volume, and environment, ensuring optimal hearing protection.

### High-quality soundfrom outside your ear.
Experience high-quality sound outside your ear with our self-developed virtual bass and dynamic EQ technology. The virtual bass algorithm reproduces lost bass bands in the headphone, resulting in a fuller and more dynamic bass. The dynamic EQ algorithm uses automatic hearing compensation to deliver even sound at different frequencies, providing a robust sound quality experience at any time, indoors or outdoors.
