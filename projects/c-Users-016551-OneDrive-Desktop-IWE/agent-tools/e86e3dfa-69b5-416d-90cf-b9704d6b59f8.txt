How do I pair OpenMeet UC with my PC?

Please follow these steps: 1. During the first pairing, the wireless adapter will automatically enter pairing mode when plugged into the PC's USB port. At this time, the adapter will flash red and blue lights alternately. 2. Put the headset into pairing mode and bring it close to the wireless adapter within 2 minutes. 3. When the headset successfully pair with the wireless adapter, the adapter will display a solid cyan light. To play music or take calls on the headset, please ensure the “Loop120 by Shokz” is both identified as the default input and output devices in your operating system’s audio settings.

How do I mute/unmute the microphone？

To mute the microphone, click the mute button once during the call. The Loop120 will show solid red. To unmute the microphone, click the mute button once during the call.

How to connect OpenMeet UC to Shokz Connect?

1. Plug Shokz Loop 120 into the PC. 2. OpenMeet pairs and connects with Shokz Loop120. 3. When the Shokz Connect interface displays "Loop120" and "OpenMeet," it means the connection was successful.

How do I reset my wireless adapter?

You can go to the Shokz Connect to reset your wireless adapter. Resetting your wireless adapter will restore your device to factory default settings and clear the Bluetooth pairing list. The wireless adapter will restart, but your firmware version will remain unchanged.

Can I use the Shokz Loop110 with my OpenMeet?

You can use Loop110 to connect with OpenMeet, but some features of Shokz Connect will not be available in OpenMeet.

How to adjust the headpiece of my OpenMeet?

You can adjust the headpiece by sliding the telescopic rod up or down.

How many sizes does the pressure relief cushion come in?

The pressure relief cushion comes in three sizes: S, M, and L.

What device and operating system does Shokz App require?

Shokz App is compatible with mobile phones only, other devices like laptops, PC, tablets PC/Pad are not supported yet. The OS of your mobile would require at least iOS13.0 / Android 8.0 or above.

How do I pair OpenMeet with my mobile device?

1. Start with your headset turned off. 2. Press and hold the Multifuction Button until the LED Indicator flashes red and blue alternately. 3. Select "OpenMeet by Shokz" on your device's Bluetooth settings.

How do I pair OpenMeet with multiple devices?

The OpenMeet come with multipoint pairing enabled by default. After connecting the first device, use the same pairing procedure to connect the second device.
