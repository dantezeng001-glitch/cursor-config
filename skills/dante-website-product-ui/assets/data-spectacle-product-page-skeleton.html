FAQ

What audio codecs are supported?
Supports LDAC, LHDC 5.0, AAC, and SBC. Hi-Res Audio Wireless certified with up to 96kHz/24bit transmission via LDAC or LHDC 5.0. Codec availability depends on your source device and OS version.
How does the adaptive ANC work?
The Intelligent ANC 3.0 system uses a dual-mic hybrid architecture (feed-forward + feedback) that scans ambient noise 200 times per second and adjusts cancellation depth in real time. Three manual modes — Deep, Comfort, Wind Shield — are available via the companion app.
What is the actual battery life?
8 hours per single charge with ANC on (LDAC, 50% volume). Up to 10 hours with ANC off. The charging case provides 5 additional charges for up to 50 hours total. 10-minute fast charge delivers approximately 4 hours of playback.
Is it compatible with both iOS and Android?
Full Bluetooth 5.3 compatibility with iOS 14+ and Android 9+. The companion app is available on both platforms. Multipoint connection supports simultaneous pairing with 2 devices.
How does the bone conduction microphone improve call quality?
The bone conduction sensor picks up vocal vibrations directly through jaw contact, bypassing ambient noise entirely. Combined with dual beamforming microphones and AI noise suppression, it delivers clear voice transmission even in wind speeds up to 40km/h.
What is the IP55 rating?
IP55 means protection against dust ingress and low-pressure water jets from any direction. Suitable for workouts and rain, but not designed for swimming or submersion.
What ear tip sizes are included?
Three sizes (S/M/L) of liquid silicone ear tips with memory foam core. The companion app includes an ear tip fit test to help you find the optimal seal for best ANC performance and comfort.
Can I customize touch controls?
Yes. Double-tap, triple-tap, and long-press actions are fully customizable via the companion app. Default mappings: double-tap for play/pause, triple-tap for next track, long-press for ANC mode toggle.
What does "Co-tuned by Dynaudio" mean?
The audio profile was collaboratively tuned with Dynaudio's acoustic engineers for balanced frequency response and natural timbre. Dynaudio participated in tuning and validation; the drivers are manufactured in-house.
